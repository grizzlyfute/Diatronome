<i>Diatronome</i> is a 3-in-1 android application. It provides a <b>tuner</b>,
a <b>visual-metronome</b>, and a <b>synthesizer</b>

-Features:
- Adjustable references pitch
- Transposition support
- Visual indicator for tuner and metronome
- Continuous synthesizer mode
- Capture tempo (central click on metronome)
- Multiple customizable settings (even the app color)
- Advanced parameters available to work on a maximum of devices.

Audio recording and playback permission are required to run.
The application provides an useful practice tools for musicians.

Keywords: metronome, tuner
