<i>Diatronome</i> is a 3-in-1 android application. It provide a <b>tuner</b>,
a <b>visual-metronome</b>, and a <b>synthesizer</b>

- Tuner have adjustable reference pitch, support transposition and have
multiple temperament and customization.
- Metronome use a visual needle indicating the timing precisely. Time and
subdivision can be set by click. Tempo can be captuered by taping on center.
Tonality is also customizable.
- Synthetizer provide continous play, different wave form and 8 octaves range.
- Application colors can be customized for a better experience.
- Advanced adjustements are available to work of maximum device.

Audio recording and playback permission are require.

The application provide a useful practice tools for musicans.
